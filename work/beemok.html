The Project

The Beemok Hospitality Company hired Buzzworthy Studio to design and develop a brand new website that beautifully showcases its portfolio of luxury hotels and world-class entertainment venues in South Carolina.This site stands as a whimsical exhibition of their opulent assortment of hotels and restaurants. As part of our brief, we made sure that upon launch, content was well-optimized for high-value keywords, the site was lightning-fast, error-free, and that it was submitted to Google for crawling.

Visual Design

We incorporated hand drawn graphics that harmonize with their logo, enhancing the brand identity. Subtle and flowy animations were integrated, mirroring the natural elements central to their branding.The use of white space, incorporating flora and fauna, and intriguing the user with playful interactions, allowed us to capture their essence on this brand.

Subtle and Fluid Animations

Animation

In the world of luxury, time is treasured. Animations on the website ensure that every second a visitor spends has value. Hover effects, gentle parallax scrolling,page transitions and interactive elements respond precisely to a user's interaction, delivering an experience that's as fluid as this brand is exceptional.
